Pan-Fried Salmon

Recipe Courtesy of Food Network


Ingredients

  • 4 5-oz. salmon filets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Fresh ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Bring salmon to room temperature
  2. In a nonstick skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-low heat.
  3. Season the fish with the salt and pepper.
  4. Raise the heat to medium-high and place the salmon in the skillet, skin side up. Cook until golden brown, then flip the fish with a spatula. Cook until fish feels firm and skin is crisp.
  5. Transfer to a plate and serve as you wish.

Notes & Suggestions

The skin can either be eaten or removed. Your preference.

Salmon can be served in a variety of different ways! Serve it up with honey mustard, mashed potatoes, and greens. Try a honey glaze and serve with rice. The possibilities are endless!


This is one of my favorite recipes because it is simple, yet full of flavor. Plus, salmon can be served with a variety of seasonings, glazes, and sides, making for a new experience every time!
